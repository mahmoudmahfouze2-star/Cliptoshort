About ClipToShort

ClipToShort is a free online video clipping tool that allows users to create short preview clips from longer video files. This tool is designed for content creators who want to convert long-form videos into short-form content quickly and easily.

Users can upload a video, choose a start time, and select the duration of the clip. The tool processes the request instantly and generates a short preview ready for sharing.

We prioritize user privacy and do not permanently store uploaded content.
